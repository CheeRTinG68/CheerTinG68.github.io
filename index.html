丁 齐 儿

Qier Ding
Moving Image Photography Graphic Design Art Direction

我的创作游走于影像、摄影与设计之间，探索表演与真实之间那片微妙的空隙。通过每一种媒介，我追问身份如何被建构、自我认知如何在压力下偏移，以及光与形式如何成为内心世界的语言。

My work moves between moving image, photography, and design — tracing the space between what is performed and what is truly felt. Through each medium I ask how identity is assembled, how self-perception shifts under pressure, and how light and form become the language of an inner life.

澳門大學 · University of Macau
哲学硕士（教育行政管理）· MPhil Education Administration
